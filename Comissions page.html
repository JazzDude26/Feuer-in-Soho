To start the commission process, I will need a thoughrough description of the subject you want illustrated, at least 3 image references and an estimate of the scope of the project as well as your budget.

Communication can be done through my mail (jazzdude26@gmail.com) or through DM's on any of my socials.

Once you send me a message or mail I will get back to you with an quote and an agreement as soon as I can, if I take any longer than 48 hours to respond, please send it again, it might have ended up in the spam folder. Approving the quote and paying the advance fee (50% of your total) means you have agreed to the terms listed below.

Once you've sent over any references and approvd the quote , I'll go on to work on the comision, of which I will give you updates throughout. At this stage I can make alterations untill you are happy with how its going. Once the comision is completed I'll send you another invoice for the remaining 50% of your fee. Once this is paid I will go on to send you the full illustration in its original resolution without a watermark. There will be an opportunity for amendments and revisions in color and lineart once the illustration is complete.

I will charge extra for higher detail work. This can be negotiated upfront.

Depending on the ammount of commissions I currently have, the complexity of the piece and buyer communication, it will take me up to four weeks. Larger additional revisions after the product is finished will be 50% of the overall commission price.

I will draw Fantasy/Furry/Mecha, tastefull nudity, light gore and most things in that ballpark.

I won't draw Pornographic content, or otherwise offensive and hatefull things. No NFTs.

I will draw in my style. I do not do style immitation.

I reserve the right to refuse any commission I am uncomfortable with, even if it isn't listed in the TOS.

You are not allowed to use my comissions commercially i.e. Merchandise, Prints, AI Training etc. All commissions fall under the license listed above. But a commercial use fee and license can be negotiated depending on the use.
