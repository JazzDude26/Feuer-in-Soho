Commissions through Ko-Fi follow a different process.
To start the commission process, I will need:

  • An assessment of your budget
  • Information of what the illustrations are for and the scope of the project
  • A thorough description of the subject you want illustrated
  • At least 3 image references


Communication can be done through my mail or through DM's on any of my socials.
Payment upfront, no refunds.
Once you've sent over any references and payment, I'll go on to work on the commission, of which I will give you updates throughout. At this stage I can make alterations until you are happy with how its going. Once the illustration is don, I will go on to send you the full illustration in its original resolution as a .png, the original .clip and as a .psd. There will be an opportunity for amendments and revisions in color and small details once the illustration is complete.
I will charge extra for higher detail work. This can be negotiated upfront.
Depending on the amount of commissions I currently have, the complexity of the piece and buyer communication, it will take me up to 6 weeks.
Larger additional revisions after the product is finished will be 50% of the overall commission price.

I will draw Fantasy/Furry/Mecha, tasteful nudity, light gore and most things in that ballpark.
I won't draw Pornographic content, or otherwise offensive and hateful things. No NFTs. You are not allowed to use my work for the use and training of LLMs or other machine learning models that might be used to generate images.
I will draw in my style. I do not do style imitation.
I reserve the right to refuse any commission I am uncomfortable with, even if it isn't listed in the TOS.
You are not allowed to use my commissions commercially i.e. Merchandise, Prints, AI Training, Game Assets etc. All commissions fall under the license listed above. But a commercial use fee and license can be negotiated depending on the use.
As the artist I keep the copyright to the work after German copyright law, and by commissioning me you recognize that.
