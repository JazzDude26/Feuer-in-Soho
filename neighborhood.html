*** Neighborhood Project ***


Developing a Street

 
        
        
          
        
        
        This is a communal worldbuilding project, where participating artists create their own character 
        and their livingspace on the street, with the endgoal being a sprawling futuristic neighborhood.
        
        If you would like to participate, read through the rules below and get creative!
        
        

        
RULES

        Be respectful towards eachother.
        
        This project may contain mature themes that are not suited for minors
            
        No hateful content or pornography, I reserve the right to refuse your entry.
            
        The setting is Sci-Fi/Cyberpunk.
        
        Only human characters.
        However your characters may have body modification that are within the scope of a society
        that has access to advanced prosthetics.
            
        You may submit as many characters as you want.
            
        All your characters may only be housed in one building.
            
        Building location will be determined by me.
        If you and a buddy want to your characters to be neigbors,
        you can send me a message and I will see what I can do.
            
        A building may only have three outside feature.
            
        Outside features can be mixed between characters residing in the same house.
        (i.e. Feature 1 from character 2; Feature 2 from character 4; Feature 3 from character 2;)
        Send me a message or make it clear which one you want, when you publish your sheet.
        
        You may use the Template below or make your own.
        If you decide to make your own, include everything from the template.
