*** Neighborhood Project ***


This is a communal worldbuilding project, where participating artists create their own character and their livingspace on the street, with the endgoal being a sprawling futuristic neighborhood.
You can click on highlighted buildings to learn more about the characters who live in them.(currently broken)
If you would like to participate, read through the rules below and get creative!

RULES

Be respectful towards eachother.

This project may contain mature themes that are not suited for minors

No hateful content or pornography, I reserve the right to refuse your entry.

The setting is Sci-Fi/Cyberpunk.

Only human characters. However your characters may have body modification that are within the scope of a society that has access to advanced prosthetics.

You may submit as many characters as you want.

All your characters may only be housed in one building.

Building location will be determined by me. If you and a buddy want to your characters to be neigbors, you can send me a message and I will see what I can do.

A building may only have three outside feature.

Outside features can be mixed between characters residing in the same house. (Feature 1 from character 2;Feature 2 from character 3;Feature 3 from character 1;) Send me a message or make it clear which one you want, when you publish your sheet.

You may use the Template below or make your own. If you decide to make your own, include everything from the template.

You will be credited on your characters page with a link leading to your entry or please provide a link to your main social media page/website instead.

The characters and art you submit are your own, you keep every right to them.

By participating, you agree that your character will be shown on my website, you can send me a message if you want a entry of yours to be removed.

If you post your entry on social media, tag me in your post, so I can see and add it here.
