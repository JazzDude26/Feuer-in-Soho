A short history of Mars


Post-Fall mars would see a human being again in the year 1444u when a USP lander touched down. Today, the spcifics of the mission are only known to scolars of martian history but in the common mouth these settlers would be called the Children of Vulkan.

With the slow reactivation of Pre-Fall structures under FirstComm, Mars would become mainly a mining colony at first. Its crust, rich in minerals, supplied the Theseus Shipyard and Cradle. Up untill the Oracle Chorus installation was dicovered, the martian people were seen as dirty blue-collar workers, digging up ore for the rest of the Sol-System.

From then on out, Mars would see rapid urbanization and setteling by the Union beurocratic body on the planet. the martian people however who had been living in its crevaces for close to 600 years now, would always be a thorn in the side of the governmental body. The martian spirit is a free one.

During the Deimos Event, in 3002u and the Siege of Mars, cells would pop up, that would support the newliy conjured Deimos-entities and engage in skirmishes against Union and local police forces. This crisis would mark the first appearance of the Martian Red Army, a democratically organized fighting force, that early on would incorporate NHPs into its ranks.

During the Hercynian Crisis, the Martian Red Army would join forces with other opposition cells and siege Union facilities on Mars and Cradle. Today they exist as a independet militia and the more patriotic martians proudly claim that they were the reason for SecComm being dissolved.

The martian people are often described as firm but friendly.
