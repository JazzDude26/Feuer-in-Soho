*** ISOPODTOBER 2023 ***





The prototype for the mechanized isopodal frame created by Group-7, a robotics company from the planet Kaktorn. 14 legs, a simple ocular module, power-leeches to the front and back to make charging easy.

Group-7's prototype being a big hit in the Kaktorn system, they shortly after got an order from the local Union DoJ/HR Leadership. They specified a fast and nimble bot that could scout and carry out small fire missions. Equipped with 7 cameras, sharp legs and a silenced 20mm cannon it would fill out all the requirements.

With the first contracts fulfilled, Group-7 looked into making their frames available for the wider public. Their first stop being bot racing. Cutting down the number of legs but upping their strength, they created a fearsome competitor. No one has won a race with this frame yet but they have consistently ranked top 5.

With the success of their stealth and racing frames, Group-7 immediately began work on a frame for heavier capacities to anticipated demand. This was the showroom unit that was mocked up to look like it had endured combat.

As they seemingly couldnt be stopped in its tracks, Group-7 started building on its heavier frame with civilian applications. While passanger transport might not have been the first obvious step from a combat or race bot, the BUS-TYPE frame was a instant hit. Allowing for public transportation in even over rough terrain that hasn't been prepared, it brought easy access transport to even the rockiest moons.
