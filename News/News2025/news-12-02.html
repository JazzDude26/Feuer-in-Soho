Another month another art.

I have been kinda taking a break towards the end of November from drawing. sometimes you need to. I played a bunch of Space Enginners. Its a pretty mediocre game but I have been playing it for the better half of a decade and am kinda excited for the sequel now. Though SE2 is already in early access its nowhere near a state where I would consider buying it, since its missing a ton of the features I love from SE1, like rotors hinges and ofc, weapons. I do just likebuilding ships that look cool and do cool stuff.

Also whats up with Hytale? I seen people talk about it again.
