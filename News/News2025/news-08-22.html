Commission

Art of the Week


People that know me creatively will have picked up that im always kinda worldbuilding with my art. I do like to make anthology esque drawings that tell you something about a setting or have some fun implications about the world they exist in.
I usually never elaborate on them with something like a lore bible or a written document. But I like the way where the story thats told is not nailed down. It gives a bit of wiggle room.
These came about after I saw a drawing by Drew Shields (Gnarly Ghost on most socials). They are very good at combining character art and landscapes, a thing that I struggle with so I mainly keep them seperated for now, but im slowly getting there.



As the doodle says, the sketch was provided by Bruzzese on Bluesky. They just put it out there for people to build upon so I did. I think the general consensus was, though not coordinated, that this was some sort of villain. I at least saw a lot of villain esque characters come out off this.



And just some doodles I did when I had time.
